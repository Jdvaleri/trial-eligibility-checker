Finding Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria in EHRs

  • Nextech: Navigate to the "Clinical" tab, select "Ophthalmology Notes," and review the "Retina" section for BCDVA, retinal findings, and ACD measurements.
  • Eyefinity: Go to "ExamWRITER," open the patient chart, and check the "Vision Assessment" and "Diagnostics" tabs for relevant data.
  • Epic: Access the patient’s "Chart Review" > "Ophthalmology" module, look under "Encounter Notes" or "Test Results" for eligibility criteria.
  • EyeMD: Open the "Patient Record," select "Exam History," and review "Visual Acuity" and "Retinal Exam" sections.

Engaging Patients In-Office

- Build rapport: Greet warmly and explain the study’s purpose briefly.
- Highlight benefits: Emphasize improved vision potential.
- Use visuals: Show a simple diagram of the procedure if available.
- Address concerns: Be ready to discuss risks and answer questions.
